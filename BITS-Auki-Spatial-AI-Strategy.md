## Use Cases by Vertical

### 🏗️ Construction & Trades (Home Base)
- Worker looks at a wall → Auki overlays exact placement to spec, Field Coach answers questions in real time
- Multiple workers see the same AR instructions simultaneously — no more miscommunication
- Before/after spatial scan verifies finished work matches blueprint to the centimeter
- Foreman sees real-time AR markers across the job site: complete, in-progress, flagged

### 📦 Warehouse & Logistics
- Workers see AR arrows pointing to exact shelf locations for pick/pack (Amazon-grade, but you own it)
- New employee onboarding — AR walks them through the warehouse step by step, no trainer needed
- Inventory management — scan with glasses, Auki maps items spatially, Field Coach updates database
- Safety zones — AR highlights restricted areas, equipment hazards, weight limits

### 🏛️ Government & Public Infrastructure
- Facility maintenance with AR overlays showing utility line routes underground
- Infrastructure inspection: AR highlights problem areas at exact coordinates
- Military/defense: facility maintenance on air-gapped, secure BITS hardware (no federal cloud waiver needed)
- Immutable blockchain audit trail: "This asset inspected at these exact coordinates on this date"

### 🛒 Retail
- Associates see AR planogram overlays — exactly where every product should be placed
- Inventory accuracy — glasses see what's on the shelf, AI flags discrepancies vs. what should be there
- New associate training — AR guides through store layout, products, procedures

### 🏥 Healthcare & Pharma
- AR-guided medication picking (accuracy-critical, liability-critical)
- Medical equipment maintenance with spatially-anchored service histories
- HIPAA-compliant by architecture — data never leaves the BITS box

### 🏭 Manufacturing
- Assembly workers see step-by-step AR overlays — quality and speed improve simultaneously
- Quality control — AI vision flags defects, Auki anchors the flag to exact location on product
- Machine maintenance guided by AR with spatially-precise component identification
