The Infrastructure Gap

The RWA tokenization market is projected to reach $400B by end of 2026 — but the industry has built the on-chain compliance layer without building the off-chain data sovereignty layer underneath it. Investor PII, fund documents, compliance records, and operational data require a privacy-preserving custody solution that keeps sensitive information sovereign while providing cryptographic proof of integrity to on-chain systems. That layer doesn't exist yet.

What DataVault Does
🔒 Privacy-First Architecture

Sensitive data never touches the blockchain. Only cryptographic hashes cross the network boundary — preserving complete document confidentiality while establishing immutable proof of existence and integrity.

📊 Operational Record Activation

Transforms dormant business records into searchable, authenticated, and economically active digital assets through AI-powered indexing and blockchain verification.

⚡ Smart Contract Automation

Embedded partnership terms auto-execute upon verified events — no manual intervention, no escrow, no renegotiation. Programmable ownership rights built in from day one.

🌐 Multi-Ledger Compatible

Blockchain-agnostic by design. Integrates with public chains, private networks, and permissioned ledgers — meeting institutional clients wherever their assets live.
