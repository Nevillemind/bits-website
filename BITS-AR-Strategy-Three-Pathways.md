# B.I.T.S. AR Strategy - Three Pathways
**Date:** November 29, 2025
**Purpose:** Evaluate strategic options for AR glasses integration

---

## Executive Decision Framework

**Question:** How should B.I.T.S. enter the AR glasses market?

## OPTION 3: Hybrid Multi-Tier Strategy ⭐ RECOMMENDED

### Strategy Overview

**Core Concept:**
- Deploy tiered approach with different hardware for different segments
- Resell proven devices initially (low risk, fast to market)
- Build custom hardware ONLY after validating demand
- Best of both worlds: speed + eventual margin

**Phases:**
1. **Phase 1 (Months 1-6):** Resell only, learn the market
2. **Phase 2 (Months 7-12):** Continue reselling + design custom hardware
3. **Phase 3 (Year 2):** Launch custom hardware alongside reselling
4. **Phase 4 (Year 3+):** Transition to primarily custom hardware
