# BITS — Year 1 Go-To-Market Strategy
*Drafted: February 23, 2026 | Confidential*

---

## The Golden Rule
The fastest B2B growth always comes from **warm networks and channel partners** — not cold ads. BITS is sitting on three of the most powerful warm networks imaginable. That's where we start.

---

## Months 1-3: Foundation & First Wins
**Goal: 5-10 clients**

### Priority #1 — Turn Pilots Into Proof
Kareem and George's brother's plumbing company aren't just pilot clients — they're the first **case studies**. Document everything:
- Hours saved per week
- Quotes closed faster
- Errors reduced
- Employee confidence up

One real number ("saved 8 hours/week on quotes") beats 10 slides of marketing copy. Get that number. Put it everywhere.

### Priority #2 — Activate the 3 Warm Networks

| Network | Who | How |
|---------|-----|-----|
| G&W Contracting | Subcontractor network — 20+ businesses who already know and trust George | Personal call/text. "I built something. Let me show you." |
| LTD/Mundin Enterprises | Business owners George coaches — already primed for growth tools | Wednesday team training presentation. Demo live. |
| VBC | Virginia blockchain/tech community — innovators and early adopters | Post-GatherVerse momentum. Strike while hot. |

### Priority #3 — LinkedIn Goes Live
Post the founder story (Option C from content doc) after GatherVerse. Follow with 2x/week posts — education first, always. "Here's what your cloud AI is doing with your data." No selling. Just truth.

---

## Months 4-6: Network Multipliers
**Goal: 25-50 clients**

### The Referral Engine
Every client who refers someone gets **$200/mo credit** toward their subscription. Clients become the sales force. Cost of acquisition: nearly zero.

### Channel Partner #1 — Regional Trade Suppliers
Suppliers that G&W subcontractors already buy from — lumber yards, concrete suppliers, electrical distributors. They see these business owners every week. BITS becomes something *they* recommend.
- Referral fee: $100-200 one-time per signup
- They have zero tech to sell — this is a gift to them

### Channel Partner #2 — Accountants & Bookkeepers
They see SMB financials. They know who's losing money on inefficiency. They have deep trust.
- Pitch: "Your clients are leaving money on the table without AI. You can be the one who fixes that."
- Referral fee applies

### Speaking Circuit Begins
GatherVerse is the template. After that:
- Richmond chamber events
- VBC quarterly events
- Trade association meetings (Virginia Building & Construction Association)
- Every speaking slot = 30 targeted buyers who hear the pitch for free

---

## Months 7-9: Content Engine & PR
**Goal: 75-100 clients**

### Content Machine
By now BITS has 3-5 case studies. Turn each one into:
- A LinkedIn post (George's voice, real numbers)
- An X thread (@bitscorpus)
- A short video testimonial if possible

One case study = 6 pieces of content. Post 3x/week minimum. Education always leads. Never pitch without teaching first.

### Local PR Push
- Richmond Times-Dispatch
- Virginia Business Magazine
- Local TV news ("Richmond entrepreneur building AI for small businesses")

These are free and reach exactly the right buyer. A journalist loves the story: contractor-turned-tech-founder helping other contractors. That's George.

### Apply for Grants & Accelerators
- Virginia Innovation Partnership Corporation (VIPC) grants
- SBA Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) program
- Virginia CIT programs
- Richmond economic development tech grants

VBC connections + George's credibility = competitive advantage in applications. Free capital while growing.

---

## Months 10-12: Systematize & Expand
**Goal: 150+ clients**

### Hire First Salesperson
By month 10, need someone making outbound calls while George focuses on vision, product, and relationships.
- Comp structure: $2K base + 15% first-year revenue (commission-heavy)
- Pays for itself with 3-4 closes/month

### Franchise/Reseller Model
Find 2-3 trusted people in other Virginia markets (Northern Virginia, Hampton Roads) as **BITS regional partners**:
- They sell and support locally
- Revenue split with BITS
- Instant geographic expansion without overhead

### Expand to Maryland & North Carolina
By month 12, planting flags in neighboring states:
- VBC's regional relationships open doors
- Maryland has a massive trades market
- NC has George's LTD/Amway mentor network — warm doors already open
