## PILOT PROGRAM OVERVIEW

### What Is the Pilot Program?

A **60-day, 50% discounted trial** of BITS services designed to:
- Prove value with minimal risk to the client
- Generate case studies and testimonials
- Refine the product based on real feedback
- Build long-term client relationships

### Pilot Program Terms

| Element | Terms |
|---------|-------|
| Duration | 60 days |
| Pricing | 50% of setup fee, 50% of monthly fee |
| Cancellation | 30-day notice, no penalties |
| Support | Full support included |
| Expectations | Weekly check-in, success metrics tracked |
| End of Pilot | Option to convert to full, return hardware, or extend |

### Success Metrics for Pilot Program

**Year 1 Goals:**
- 10 pilot clients launched
- 60% conversion to full paying clients
- 3 published case studies
- $150,000+ in pilot revenue (converts to $300K+ full)

### Messaging Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Lead with the problem (data privacy risk)
- Use specific numbers and examples
- Keep it simple (avoid technical jargon)
- Be authentic and transparent
- Follow up persistently but politely
- Personalize every message

**DON'T:**
- Lead with "blockchain" (sounds complex/risky)
- Use buzzwords without explanation
- Make claims you can't back up
- Send generic mass emails
- Be pushy or aggressive
- Over-promise on capabilities

## SALES PROCESS

### Stage 1: Qualification (First Call - 15 minutes)

**Goals:**
- Confirm they're a good fit
- Identify decision maker
- Understand current AI usage
- Assess urgency

**Key Questions:**

```
1. "Tell me about how your team currently uses AI tools."
2. "Has anyone raised concerns about data privacy or compliance?"
3. "What's your current monthly spend on AI or cloud services?"
4. "Who would be involved in a decision like this?"
5. "What's your timeline for evaluating solutions like this?"
```

**Qualifying Criteria:**

| Criterion | Qualified If |
|-----------|--------------|
| AI Usage | Currently using or actively evaluating |
| Pain | Acknowledges privacy/compliance concern |
| Budget | Can afford $15K+ investment |
| Timeline | Decision within 90 days |
| Authority | Can introduce to decision maker |

**Red Flags:**
- "We're not using AI at all" → Long education cycle
- "Price is our only concern" → Not our target customer
- "We already have a solution" → Ask what it is, might be complementary
- "IT says no" → Need to find a champion

---

### Stage 2: Discovery (Deep Dive - 45 minutes)

**Goals:**
- Understand their technical environment
- Identify specific use cases
- Map stakeholders
- Build ROI case

**Agenda:**

```
1. Current State (10 min)

   - Data types and sensitivity

2. Use Case Identification (15 min)

   - On-site requirements
   - Integration needs

4. Business Case (10 min)
   - ROI calculation
```

## CLOSING STRATEGIES

### Strategy 1: The "No-Brainer" Pilot Close

**Framework:**
1. Standard setup: $7,500
2. Pilot setup: $3,750 (50% off)
3. If they convert: Apply remaining $3,750 to full agreement
4. Result: No additional cost to pilot

**Script:**
> "Here's what makes this a no-brainer: The pilot is 50% off. If you love it and convert, we simply apply the remaining 50% to your full agreement. If you don't love it, we take the hardware back (though most clients want to keep it). Worst case, you're out $3,750 for a thorough evaluation. Best case, you've found your AI solution."

### Strategy 2: The "Limited Availability" Close

**Framework:**
1. Only 10 pilot slots per quarter
2. Creates urgency
3. First-come, first-served

**Script:**
> "We're only accepting 10 pilot participants this quarter to ensure we can give each one proper attention. We've already committed 6 spots. Would you like to reserve one of the remaining 4?"

### Strategy 3: The "Case Study" Close

**Framework:**
1. Pilot participants get discounted pricing
2. In exchange for case study participation
3. Win-win: client saves, BITS gets social proof

**Script:**
> "Here's the deal: We're looking for pilot clients who would be willing to participate in a case study if the pilot is successful. In exchange, you get 50% off. We've found that companies who see great results are happy to share their story. Does that sound fair?"

### Strategy 4: The "Comparison" Close

**Framework:**
1. Compare pilot cost to 1-2 months of current spend
2. Low-risk evaluation

**Script:**
> "Your current AI spend is $X/month. The 2-month pilot costs $Y. You're basically redirecting 1 month of your current spend to thoroughly evaluate a better alternative. And if it works, you'll save $Z/month thereafter."

### Strategy 5: The "Option" Close

**Framework:**
1. Present 2-3 options
2. Let them choose
3. All options lead to yes

**Script:**
> "Based on our conversation, I see three paths forward:
> 1. Pilot the Professional tier at $3,750 setup
> 2. Pilot the Starter tier at $1,500 setup
> 3. Go straight to full implementation at standard pricing
>
> Which option feels right for where you are right now?"
