The Problem We Solve

Every day, workers in the field face situations where they need an answer — and the expert is back at the office, the manual is in the truck, or the foreman is on another job. That gap between "I need to know" and "I can find out" costs businesses thousands in delays, mistakes, and rework.

BITS Field Coach closes that gap. Your organization's knowledge — SOPs, procedures, specifications, safety protocols — becomes an AI-powered assistant that every worker can query in real time, by voice, hands-free.

How It Works

1

Capture Your Knowledge

We work with your team to digitize institutional knowledge — standard operating procedures, best practices, safety protocols, equipment manuals — into an AI-queryable knowledge base tailored to your operations.

2

Deploy on Your Hardware

Field Coach runs entirely on a local BITS Box — a pre-configured device that sits in your facility. No cloud dependency. No data leaving your building. Your knowledge stays yours.

3

Workers Ask, AI Answers

Your team queries Field Coach by voice — through smart glasses, a mobile device, or any connected device. "What's the spec for this?" "What's the safety protocol?" Instant, accurate answers drawn from YOUR knowledge base.

4

Supervisors See Everything

The supervisor dashboard shows what workers are asking, where knowledge gaps exist, and how the system is being used — giving management real-time intelligence on field operations without micromanaging.
