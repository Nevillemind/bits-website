How TRACE Works

Five steps. Fully automatic. No human authorization required at any point.

1
Capture
Wearable device, body cam, drone, robot, or phone captures evidence in the field
2
Hash
Raw data cryptographically fingerprinted BEFORE any AI, compression, or human review
3
Sign
Hardware security chip signs the fingerprint with a key that can never be copied or extracted
4
Anchor
Signed proof recorded automatically on Solana mainnet — permanent, public, irrevocable
5
Verify
Anyone — judge, attorney, regulator — independently verifies authenticity without trusting any party

Core Capabilities

🔐

Hardware-Level Proof-of-Origin

TRACE's breakthrough innovation: a cryptographic proof that answers the deepest question in digital evidence law — "Did this data come from a real sensor, or was it AI-generated?" The evidence hash is computed before any processing, signed by a tamper-resistant hardware key that never leaves the device. This is the deepfake defense no existing system provides.

Automatic Solana Mainnet Anchoring

Every capture automatically submits a memo transaction to Solana mainnet — no human approval needed. The resulting transaction signature is the permanent, public proof of existence.
