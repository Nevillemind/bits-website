## PRICING PHILOSOPHY

### Core Principles

1. **Asset Ownership First**
   - Clients own hardware (their asset on their balance sheet)
   - We're not selling subscriptions for rented equipment
   - Clear separation between one-time costs and ongoing value

2. **Value-Based Pricing**
   - Price based on value delivered, not cost-plus
   - Compliance, data sovereignty, and independence have premium value
   - Cost savings relative to alternatives are clear and quantifiable

3. **Predictable Revenue**
   - Monthly management fees are fixed and predictable
   - No surprise charges or usage-based overages
   - Clients can budget accurately

4. **Fair Margins**
   - Hardware: 15-25% margin (covers procurement, warranty handling)
   - Services: 70-90% margin (covers expertise, time)
   - Monthly: 60-75% margin after support costs

## PRICING STRATEGIES BY CUSTOMER TYPE

### Strategy 1: The Compliance Buyer

**Target:** Healthcare, legal, finance, government contractors

**Pricing Approach:** Premium pricing, compliance value
- Add 20-30% premium for compliance features
- Emphasize risk avoidance value
- Include compliance reporting and audits
- Offer higher-tier support with SLA guarantees

**Example Pricing:**
```
HIPAA-Compliant AI Package:
- Base Professional Package: $28,500 + $795/mo
- Compliance Premium: +30%
- Final Price: $37,050 + $1,035/mo
- Includes: BAA documentation, audit logs, annual compliance review
```

### Strategy 2: The Cost-Conscious Buyer

**Target:** Small businesses, startups, budget-conscious

**Pricing Approach:** Competitive pricing, emphasize ROI
- Lower margins on hardware (10-15%)
- Standard pricing on services
- Emphasize break-even timeline
- Offer financing or payment plans

**Example Pricing:**
```
Budget AI Package:
- Hardware at cost: $15,000 (instead of $17,500)
- Setup: Standard $7,500
- Monthly: $495 (basic support instead of professional)
- Total: $22,500 + $495/mo (21% discount)
```

### Strategy 3: The Strategic Buyer

**Target:** Mid-size companies evaluating strategic alternatives

**Pricing Approach:** Value-based, competitive positioning
- Compare directly to current spend
- Show 3-5 year TCO comparison
- Bundle multiple products for discount
- Strategic partnership pricing

**Example Pricing:**
```
Strategic Independence Bundle:
- AI Professional + Workspace Professional + Edge
- Separately: $55,000 + $1,495/mo
- Bundle Discount: 15%
- Final Price: $46,750 + $1,270/mo
- Includes: Executive sponsorship, quarterly reviews, roadmap input

### Strategy 4: The Enterprise Buyer

**Target:** Large organizations, multi-location

**Pricing Approach:** Custom pricing, volume discounts
- Site licensing instead of per-location
- Enterprise agreement with volume tiers
- Professional services separate from product
- Long-term contract incentives

**Example Pricing:**
```

- Custom hardware package: $250,000
- Professional services: $100,000
- Monthly: $5,000 (all-inclusive)

- Multi-location: 10% | 3+ locations simultaneously

### Negotiation Playbook

**When they say "It's too expensive":**
```
"I understand budget is a concern. Let me show you the 3-year cost
comparison. While the upfront investment is higher, your monthly
costs drop significantly. Over 3 years, you'll actually save $X
compared to your current approach while gaining complete data
ownership and compliance benefits."
```

**When they ask for a big discount (20%+):**
```
"I want to make this work for you. Here's what I can do:
- If you commit to a 3-year agreement, I can offer 5%
- If you pre-pay annually, that's another 5%
- If you sign this month, I'll add an extra 5%

That's 15% total - the maximum I can offer without additional
commitment. Would any of those work for you?"
```

**When they compare to cheaper alternatives:**
```
"I understand there are lower-cost options. Here's what makes us
different:

1. We include hardware - some alternatives require you to buy separately
2. Our support is included - others charge extra
3. We handle everything - alternatives require DIY
4. Data sovereignty - alternatives may still send data externally

When you compare apples to apples, our pricing is very competitive
for the value we deliver."
```

## PRICING FAQ

**Q: Why is the upfront cost higher than alternatives?**

A: "You're purchasing an asset, not renting access. Like buying vs. leasing a car, ownership costs more upfront but delivers long-term savings. Plus, you own the hardware - it's on your balance sheet."

**Q: Can I pay monthly for everything?**

A: "We offer financing through partners for qualified buyers. Or we can structure a 3-year agreement where hardware is included in monthly payments (with interest)."

**Q: What if I outgrow the hardware?**

A: "We offer trade-in credits. Also, our Professional tier is designed for 10-50 users with room to grow. We'll help you right-size the initial purchase."

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**

A: "None. Your quote includes everything: hardware, setup, training, and the first month of support. The only ongoing cost is your monthly management fee, which is fixed."

**Q: Do you offer annual payment discounts?**

A: "Yes! Pay annually and get 2 months free (17% discount)."

**Q: What if I need to cancel?**

A: "You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. You own the hardware - it's yours to keep. We'll even help you transition to self-management if desired."
