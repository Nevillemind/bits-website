The Problem With Cloud AI

Every time your team uses ChatGPT, Copilot, or any cloud AI service, your business data — questions, documents, client information, internal processes — leaves your building and goes to someone else's servers. You don't control it. You can't audit it. And you're paying a monthly subscription for the privilege.

The BITS AI Assistant Box changes that equation entirely. It's a physical device — small enough to sit on a shelf — that runs the same caliber of AI models locally. Your data never leaves. There's no per-user subscription to big tech. And it's yours.

How It Works

It Ships to You, Ready to Go

We pre-configure your BITS Box with AI models, your BITS products (Field Coach, DataVault, etc.), and remote management tools. It arrives ready to plug in — no IT team required. Connect it to power and your network, and you're live.

Enterprise AI, Running Locally

The Box runs state-of-the-art open-source AI models — the same quality you'd get from cloud services — entirely on your hardware. Your team asks questions, generates documents, analyzes data, and gets AI assistance without anything leaving your building.

Remotely Managed & Updated

BITS manages your Box remotely — software updates, model upgrades, troubleshooting, and new feature deployment. You get the convenience of a managed service with the privacy of on-premise hardware. We see the system, never your data.

All BITS Products, One Device

Field Coach, DataVault, Halo, Freedom AI, Freedom Workspace — every BITS product runs on the same Box. As your needs grow, we deploy new capabilities remotely. One device. Unlimited potential.
