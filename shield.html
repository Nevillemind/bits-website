Core Capabilities

Dual-Hash Technology — Deepfake Defense

TRACE Shield's breakthrough: two fingerprints, two layers of protection. The SHA-256 hash catches any exact copy — any byte changed, any edit made. The perceptual hash catches what SHA-256 misses — the re-encoded version, the AI-regenerated face swap, the slightly cropped repost. Together they create a detection net that no manipulation technique can slip through.

Automatic Solana Blockchain Anchoring

Every verification automatically submits both hashes as a memo transaction to Solana mainnet — no human approval needed. The resulting transaction signature is your permanent, public, irrevocable timestamp of creation. Anyone can verify it on Solscan.io in seconds. Your proof of ownership begins the moment you upload.

Verifiable Certificates with QR Codes

Every anchored piece of content receives a professionally formatted verification certificate containing the content hash, blockchain transaction ID, timestamp, and a QR code. Anyone — a potential buyer, a media outlet, a court — can scan the QR code and instantly verify the content's authenticity against the Solana blockchain record.
