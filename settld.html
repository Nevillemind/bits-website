Core Capabilities

📍

GPS Geofenced Checkpoint Verification

Settld uses the Haversine distance formula to calculate precise GPS proximity at each checkpoint. When a carrier or contractor enters the geofenced zone — delivery dock, job site, inspection point — verification triggers automatically. No manual confirmation. No "I was there" disputes. Mathematics confirms presence.

🔒

Blockchain Escrow — Funds Locked Until Proof

Payment is locked in smart contract escrow the moment a job is created. Funds cannot be diverted, withheld, or disputed away. When GPS and photo proof confirm completion, the escrow releases automatically — no human approval needed, no accounts payable bottleneck, no net-90 terms. The contract executes itself.

📸

Photo Proof with SHA-256 Hash + TRACE Integration

Every checkpoint requires photographic proof. The photo is SHA-256 hashed and anchored to Solana via TRACE — the same blockchain authentication that powers BITS TRACE and TRACE Shield. Every proof of completion is immutable, timestamped, and independently verifiable. Disputes collapse against this evidence.

⚡

Two Payment Modes — Instant and Streaming

Instant: Full payment releases on final delivery confirmation — perfect for freight and simple gig deliveries. Streaming: Milestone-based payment streams release as each checkpoint is verified — perfect for construction phases, multi-stop routes, and long-duration contracts. Choose the model that fits the work.

⛓️

TRACE Integration — Every Proof Blockchain-Anchored

Settld is built on BITS' TRACE infrastructure. Every GPS checkpoint, every photo proof, every completion event is anchored to Solana mainnet via TRACE. This means Settld payments aren't just fast — they're audit-proof. Every transaction comes with an immutable, court-ready record of what was done, when, and by whom.
