The Vision

In every high-stakes environment — a football field, an operating room, a tactical operation, a racing circuit — human operators must process massive amounts of information under extreme pressure and time compression. The gap between what they need to know and what they can process in the moment is where mistakes happen.

BITS Halo closes that gap. It delivers AI-powered cognitive intelligence directly to operators through wearable eyewear and audio — personalized to their role, relevant to the moment, and available without taking their hands off the task or their eyes off the environment.

Core Capabilities

AI Auto-Translation

A commander, coach, or lead surgeon issues one directive — Halo's AI automatically translates it into personalized, role-specific instructions for every team member simultaneously. One command. Every operator gets exactly what they need to execute.

Audio-Primary Architecture

Audio is the primary intelligence channel — always on, always reliable. Visual HUD display provides supplementary information. If conditions compromise the display (fog, rain, glare, sweat), the system continues delivering full intelligence through audio without interruption.

Operator Eye-Scan Intelligence

The operator's natural visual scanning — looking left to right across a field, scanning a room, assessing an environment — feeds the AI in real time. Halo interprets what the operator is seeing and provides contextually relevant intelligence based on their directed attention.

Platform-Agnostic & Future-Proof

Halo runs on any wearable hardware from any manufacturer through a hardware abstraction layer. When new eyewear or sensor technology enters the market — thermal, infrared, LIDAR, or capabilities not yet invented — a software update integrates it. No rebuilds. No replacements.

Encrypted & Secure

All communications are encrypted end-to-end. Intelligence routing between command and operators cannot be intercepted, decoded, or replayed. Authority override capability allows officials to control communication during designated periods.

Domain Applications

Sports

Coach calls one play — every player hears their specific assignment in their ear. The quarterback's camera reads the defense in real time. AI identifies formations and alerts the team before the snap. Football, baseball, rugby, hockey, and beyond.

Military & Tactical

Commander issues a tactical directive — each operator receives their specific role assignment. AI analyzes the environment through helmet-mounted sensors. Threat identification, building intelligence, and team coordination in real time.

Medical & Surgical

Lead surgeon calls a procedure step — each team member hears their specific action. AI identifies anatomical structures, references medical literature on voice query, and monitors patient vitals for proactive alerts.

Motorsports

Race engineer sends a strategy change — the driver sees it on their visor HUD while each pit crew member hears their preparation task. AI monitors tire degradation, competitor gaps, and weather conditions in real time.

Law Enforcement

Incident commander assigns perimeter positions — each officer receives their specific coverage zone. AI cross-references addresses, identifies building layouts, and provides real-time situational intelligence during active operations.

Emergency Response

Incident commander directs a structure fire operation — attack teams, search and rescue, ventilation, and rapid intervention each receive role-specific instructions. AI monitors structural integrity, air supply, and thermal conditions.
