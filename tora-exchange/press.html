Company Overview

TORA Exchange is the world's first marketplace for Tokenized Operational Record Assets — blockchain-verified, anonymized datasets derived from real business operations. Powered by Blockchain Integrative Technology Solutions (BITS), TORA Exchange enables businesses of any size to monetize decades of dormant records while maintaining full data sovereignty.

Built on the Solana blockchain, every data asset on the exchange is cryptographically hashed, timestamped, and verifiable. Revenue flows to data suppliers automatically via smart contract — no middlemen, no delays.
