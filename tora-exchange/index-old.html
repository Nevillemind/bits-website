How It Works
From Dormant Records
to Tradeable Assets
Three steps to transform decades of operational data into a blockchain-verified, licensable asset class.
Step 01
📦

Digitize & Authenticate

Physical and digital records are scanned, processed through AI-powered indexing, and cryptographically hashed. Every document receives blockchain-anchored proof of existence — without any content ever touching the chain.

Step 02
🔗

Tokenize as TORA

Your authenticated corpus becomes a Tokenized Operational Record Asset — a new asset class with embedded smart contracts, programmable ownership rights, and automated partnership terms. Patent pending.

Step 03
💰

List & License

Anonymized data intelligence from your TORA is listed on the Exchange. Institutional buyers license access. Revenue flows automatically through smart contracts — no intermediaries, no invoicing, no delays.
