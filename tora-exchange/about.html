Our Story

Born from a simple belief — your data is an asset. Let's unlock it.

The Problem

TORA Exchange was founded by Blockchain Integrative Technology Solutions (BITS) with a simple belief: every business is sitting on a hidden asset — their operational data.

For decades, only large corporations had the infrastructure to monetize their data. Small and mid-size businesses generated valuable records every day — invoices, service histories, compliance logs, inspection reports — but had no way to turn that data into revenue.

📊

Data Is Power

Your records contain the patterns, trends, and insights that drive decisions — but only if you can unlock them.

The Solution

TORA Exchange changes that. We built the first licensed data marketplace where businesses of any size can tokenize their operational records, list anonymized data intelligence, and earn recurring revenue — automatically, via smart contract.

🔄

Data → Revenue

Tokenize your records → List on the marketplace → Earn revenue automatically through smart contract.

How It Works

Three simple steps to turn your records into revenue.

01

Tokenize

Your business records are digitized and hashed through BITS DataVault. Documents never touch the blockchain — only cryptographic proofs.

02

List

You select which data categories to share. Anonymized, authenticated data intelligence appears on TORA Exchange, searchable by industry and region.

03

Earn

When a buyer licenses your data, the smart contract automatically sends 75-90% of the revenue directly to your wallet — no invoicing, no waiting.
