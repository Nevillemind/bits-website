Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

Our Commitment

TORA Exchange is built on the principle of data sovereignty. We believe your data belongs to you — and our privacy practices reflect that.

Key Principles

  • Data suppliers retain full ownership of their raw documents at all times. Only anonymized data intelligence is listed on the exchange.
  • No raw documents are stored on our servers or on the blockchain. Only cryptographic hashes (digital fingerprints) are recorded on-chain.
  • Buyer data — account information is used solely for platform access, licensing, and communication. We do not sell buyer information to third parties.
  • Blockchain transparency — All transactions are recorded on the Solana blockchain and are publicly verifiable.
  • No tracking for advertising — We do not use your data for targeted advertising or sell it to ad networks.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate the platform:

  • Account Information — Name, email address, company name, and wallet address when you register.
  • Transaction Data — License purchases, listings, and payment records processed via Solana smart contracts.
  • Usage Data — Basic analytics about how you interact with the platform (pages visited, features used) to improve the experience.
  • Communication Data — Messages sent through our contact form or support channels.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the TORA Exchange platform
  • To process transactions and enforce smart contract terms
  • To communicate with you about your account, listings, or licenses
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

What We Never Do

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share supplier data with buyers beyond what suppliers explicitly authorize
  • Store raw documents on our servers — only cryptographic hashes

Data Retention

Account information is retained while your account is active. Transaction records on the blockchain are permanent and immutable by design. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Deactivate your listings at any time (suppliers)
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Security

We employ industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), secure infrastructure, and access controls. Blockchain-anchored data is inherently tamper-evident through cryptographic verification.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or platform notification. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
