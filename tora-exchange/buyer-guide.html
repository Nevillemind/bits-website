Buyer Guide

Access Licensed Operational Intelligence from Real Businesses

What Is TORA Data?

TORA (Tokenized Operational Record Assets) are anonymized, blockchain-authenticated datasets derived from real business operations. Every record has been cryptographically verified — you can trust the data because it's anchored on-chain.

Who Buys TORA Data?

Decision-makers across industries use TORA data for better intelligence.

Insurance Companies

Claims patterns, risk analysis, underwriting intelligence from real operational data.

Equipment Manufacturers

Lifecycle data, failure rates, maintenance patterns across brands and regions.

Private Equity Firms

Operational due diligence data for acquisition targets and portfolio companies.

Real Estate Investors

Property maintenance histories, renovation costs, vendor performance data.

Government Agencies

Infrastructure and compliance data, regulatory benchmarking, policy analysis.

Supply Chain Companies

Vendor performance data, logistics patterns, supplier reliability metrics.

Researchers & Universities

Industry trend analysis, longitudinal studies, operational pattern research.
