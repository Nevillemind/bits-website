## Market Opportunity

### $4.8B — Total Addressable Market (TAM)
All US trade companies with 5+ workers.
~$99/worker/month across 7M+ field workers in the US.
Trades: HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Masonry, Fire Protection, General Contracting, Warehousing.

### $480M — Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
US SMB trade companies with 5–100 workers (~10% of TAM).
The segment BITS can realistically serve in the near term.
Focus: Richmond VA → Virginia → Mid-Atlantic → National.

### $48M — Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
Year 3 target: 1% of SAM.
~400 companies at ~$10K ARR average.
Achievable with strong pilot results and word-of-mouth in trade verticals.

## Beyond Contractors — Expansion Vision

Every industry where workers carry knowledge in their heads is a target:

- 🏗️ **Contracting** — Commercial & residential project management
- 📦 **Logistics** — Warehouse ops, fleet, fulfillment
- 🏛️ **Government** — Virginia + federal procurement gateway
- 🏭 **Manufacturing** — Factory floor SOPs and safety compliance

## Why BITS Wins

The formula that makes this defensible:
1. **Daily pain solved** — workers get answers in 4 seconds
2. **Institutional knowledge captured** — company SOPs and history live in Field Coach
3. **Gets smarter over time** — every job, every photo, every question improves it

Competitors can copy the AI model. They cannot copy 2 years of a company's job-site
history, custom SOPs, worker profiles, and a vision model trained on their specific trade.

**The data is the moat. And the customer owns it.**
